1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Space Shooter: Galaxy War ("the App", "the Game", "we", "our", or "us"), published by Şifreli Eğitim.

The App is an arcade space shooter game. It does not require an account, login, profile, email address, phone number, contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, phone call access, or precise location access.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store gameplay-related information locally on your device so the game can function correctly. This may include:

  • Game progress
  • Coin balance
  • Upgrade and ship-related progress
  • Game settings
  • Ad reward cooldown status
  • Purchase entitlement status, such as whether ads have been removed
  • Local purchase delivery records used to avoid granting the same consumable purchase more than once

This gameplay information is stored locally on your device. The App does not provide developer-operated cloud sync, developer-operated cloud backup, user accounts, or an online profile system.

If you delete the App, clear the App data, reset your device, or move to a new device, locally stored gameplay data may be lost.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to show ads, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads.

Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share certain data for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes. This may include:

  • IP address, which may be used to estimate general location
  • App interactions, such as app launches, taps, ad impressions, and video views
  • Diagnostic information, such as app and SDK performance data
  • Device or account identifiers, such as the Android Advertising ID, App Set ID, and related identifiers where available

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your region, device settings, consent choices, and Google advertising settings.

4. Consent Management

The App may use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request, store, and apply your advertising consent choices where required by law or platform policy.

You may also manage advertising preferences through your Android device settings and Google advertising settings.

5. In-App Purchases

The App uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Available purchases may include removing ads and buying consumable coin packs.

Payment processing is handled by Google Play. We do not collect or store your credit card number, bank information, or other payment account details.

The App may receive purchase-related information from Google Play and store purchase status locally on your device so that purchased features can work correctly. Google Play may separately process purchase-related information according to Google's own policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not operate our own backend server for this game and we do not sell your personal information.

However, third-party services used by the App may process data as described in their own policies. These services include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play services

Google's Privacy Policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Permissions

The App may request or use permissions required for gameplay, ads, billing, consent management, and basic app operation. These may include:

  • Internet and network state access, used by ads, consent, billing, and Google services
  • Vibration, used for gameplay feedback
  • Google Play Billing, used for in-app purchases
  • Advertising-related identifiers, used by Google Mobile Ads where available and permitted

The App does not request camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, phone call, or precise location permissions.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at sifreliders@gmail.com so we can review the situation.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay data stored by the App remains on your device until you delete it, clear App data, or uninstall the App.

Because the App does not use developer-operated accounts or cloud storage, we cannot remotely restore, export, or delete local gameplay data from your device. You can delete local App data through your Android device settings.

Data processed by Google services, such as advertising, consent, diagnostics, and purchase-related data, is handled according to Google's own retention and deletion policies.

Account deletion note: The App does not provide account creation or login. Therefore, there is no developer-operated user account to delete.

10. Security

We aim to limit the App's permissions and data handling to what is needed for gameplay, ads, consent management, and in-app purchases.

Data transmitted by Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using Transport Layer Security (TLS), according to Google's Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure documentation.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
